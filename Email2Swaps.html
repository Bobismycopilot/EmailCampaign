The number one reason artists don't launch their first swap? They're not sure what to offer.

Here's the thing — it doesn't need to be complicated. Some of the most effective swaps are the simplest ones.

Here are the three swap types and what artists actually use them for:

📋 Grow Contact List

Collect emails, phone numbers, and location data from fans. Use it to reach them directly, anywhere, forever. GAWNE dropped an exclusive one-take video and used a contact list swap to collect fan emails before the release. Fans were floored.

💰 Make Money

Set a price and let fans pay to access your content. An unreleased track, a studio session video, a behind-the-scenes clip — if it's exclusive, fans will pay for it.

🗳️ Get Fan Input

Let fans vote on merch designs, album covers, audio files, and more. Great for involving your community in decisions you're already making — whether that be a new merch design, cover art, which demo is better, which song to release next, or whatever you want feedback on!

Start simple — you can always build from there.
